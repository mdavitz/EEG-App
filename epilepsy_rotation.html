Daily Routines in the Epilepsy Rotation

AM

Activity Description
Long Term EEG Reading Start reading long term EEGs between 7–8 AM; prepare preliminary reports before rounding (~9–10 AM).
ICU Studies Begin with ICU studies. NSICU fellow provides names via EPIC chat for priority readings.
Rounding Round with the attending between 9–10 AM; finalize EEG reports and communicate with the primary team.

PM

Activity Description
Routine EEG Reading Read inpatient routine & baseline long-term EEGs up to 5 PM and leave preliminary reports as incomplete.
Follow Up Review all long-term EEGs (including EMU patients) and add interim progress notes if there are changes.
Discontinued EEGs On Mondays (or after discontinuations), finalize and sign off on EEGs as needed.

EMU Elective Admissions & Stay

Responsibility Details
Admissions Write H&P, place admissions orders, and sign out to on-call residents. Assist when a dedicated resident is assigned. Verify admission details (coordinate with Vincent Palmieri) and, for after-5 PM admissions, pend a preliminary H&P and detailed sign-out.

Epilepsy Consults

Consult Type Details
Consults NSICU and General Neurology teams may request consults. Fellows are to see patients, write consult notes, and staff with the attending. When rotating residents are present, consults are divided at the fellow’s discretion.
